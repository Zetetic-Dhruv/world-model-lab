## Facts

- 1.28M DFT relaxations of adsorbate-catalyst systems (PBE functional); published 4-way ID/OOD-ads/OOD-cat/OOD-both validation split.

## Hypotheses

1. OOD-both is a genuine compositional shift rather than interpolation in the (ads × cat) Cartesian product space.
2. The DFT functional choice acts as a hidden env factor, invisible inside OC20 but exposed only when paired with OC22.

## Intuitions

1. Open Catalyst likely appears in the counterfactual molecular / causal-discovery literature of 2024–25.
2. Meta FAIR probably has post-OC22 releases (a rumored OC25 or successor — status to confirm).

## Unknowns

1. The simulator-to-real-experiment gap is itself a candidate shift type, but there is no established label for "simulation-to-experiment transitions" and no primitive in the schema for crossing it.
2. Whether "catalyst" should be an atom-list or a bond-graph is a hidden representational choice the data presupposes.

## Boundary

Simulator origin — claims about real-world OOD must lean on external chemical literature, never on OC20 alone.

## Representational commitments

- PBE exchange-correlation functional fixed
- Catalyst represented as element-list + Miller index; bond-graph structure not exposed
- Adsorbate-catalyst pair is the unit; multi-adsorbate configurations excluded
- Relaxation-as-trajectory framing presupposes Born-Oppenheimer surface
